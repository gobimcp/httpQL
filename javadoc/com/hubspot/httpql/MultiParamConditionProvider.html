MultiParamConditionProvider¶

public abstract class MultiParamConditionProvider<T> extends ConditionProvider<T>¶

Like ConditionProvider for filters that require multi-value parameters.

Filters utilizing these types of Condition are not currently compatible with ParamType.NAMED.
